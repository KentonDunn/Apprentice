Apprentice Developer

Hello World! I'm currently a student at the UNC Charlotte Coding Bootcamp. I began the program with no experience in coding, so this has been a very challenging and rewarding journey. I've put in months of work and very long days and hours to add these competencies to my skillset.

My goal, upon completion of the course, is to be in a great position to secure a job as a full stack developer or as a product manager in the financial services industry. I'm currently a Payables Account Manager with Wells Fargo. I see how technologies such as APIs are expanding the financial services industry, so I'd like to take part in creating new products. I'm married with two young daughters whom I love very much and am striving to make a better life for not only myself, but also for them.
